More targeted marketing & sales campaigns

Giving business users direct and instantaneous access to fresh network performance and competitive benchmarking data – and answers they need when they need them.

The radio frequency test data that RANlytics collects contains rich information that is relevant and useful to business users . . . if they could easily access and understand it.

Cerno makes performance and benchmark test data easily and instantly accessible to everyone in your organisation who wishes to access it.

Our intuitive map interface allows anyone to start at a city, state or even country level, and zoom in to any location required – so that they can see how your networks are performing relative to your competitors’, down to the individual address or premises level.

And our test data can be easily combined with demographic data, marketing information, etc.

So, Marketing professionals can better plan localised campaigns that reflect a clear understanding of where competitors’ network coverage is weak; whilst a salesperson can rapidly assess your network coverage at their customer’s facility before a sales call.

Cerno lets business users easily & quickly find and understand what’s important to them, and what your customers’ experience is at any given location.
