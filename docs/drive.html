RANlytics Cerno Outdoor Macro Cell (Drive) Test Solution

Better RF performance & benchmarking data, collected more extensively & frequently, available to your entire organisation and accessible through easier to use and more powerful analysis tools – all at lower cost

The Problems

Collecting and post-processing macro RF performance & benchmarking data, and then deriving critical or useful information from it has always been difficult, time-consuming, expensive and resource-intensive.

The high cost of legacy test equipment and post-processing systems, plus their reliance on skilled RF operators and their fragility & poor reliability restricts your ability to test as extensively or as frequently as you need to.

The limitations imposed by traditional RF data processing systems make it difficult for engineers to distil the key information needed to drive design & optimisation, or to identify potential issues; and to provide non-technical users with information they need to drive the business.

The RANlytics Solution

Cerno has been designed and built from the ground up to address each and every one of these issues and constraints.

Using our custom-built and highly reliable Cerno RF testers we can test drive more areas, more extensively and more frequently.

Log files are uploaded to our cloud for instantaneous post-processing.

Our customers can immediately and easily locate and use the right data through our web-based Cerno Workbench. Our integrated suite of intuitive mapping, data visualisation, reporting, querying and analytics tools allow engineers to quickly find and use the information they need, when they need it.

Key Features

All RF and parameter data from all technologies and bands is collected, processed and made available
All TDD & FDD LTE bands, & all UMTS bands are covered
All RF and parameter metrics from layers 1, 2 & 3 are recorded directly from the Qualcomm chipset
Complete RAN analysis – including coverage, data & voice quality, noise, interference, overshooting, pilot pollution, neighbouring cell metrics, etc.
All technologies – including VoLTE and Carrier Aggregation – are supported
Inbuilt support for scripted data download and upload tests and voice call testing with ability to execute multiple test scripts on multiple technology/band combinations concurrently
RF performance and competitive benchmark data can be collected in a single sweep
Pervasive area coverage: every city, every regional centre, every town, every main & minor road, every street
Drone-based testing of rural areas, stadiums, etc.
Intuitive map-based data interface: easily move from seeing macro country level data to streets and individual premises
All test data ever recorded is immediately and always available – so that changes over time can be easily understood
See RF parameters at any given location, and at every premise
Inbuilt report-writer: easily customise and generate reports whenever required
Rich data visualisation filtering – filter by carrier, technology, band, serving cell, signal quality, noise, etc.
Powerful inbuilt plotting, reporting and mapping functions
Inbuilt analytics engine – set Cerno to automatically identify anomalies and flag them to your engineers
