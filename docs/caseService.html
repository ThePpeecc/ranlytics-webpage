Continuous, real-time DAS RF monitoring – true RF service assurance enabled for the first time, giving operators, neutral hosts and DAS customers accurate insight into end user experience

Implementing true service assurance models based on real-time measurements of actual RF performance rather than on inferences or estimates supports better management of SLA conformance

Each Stratus Probe collects all data from layers 1, 2 & 3 direct from the chipset and relays it to the RANlytics Cerno post-processing engine. You can continuously monitor the results in real-time via the Cerno dashboard.

Anomalies can be automatically detected and flagged to you as they occur.

As with all of RANlytics’s solutions, all test data is retained permanently, allowing root causes of issues and faults to be identified to the minute.

Continuously monitor the health of either passive or active DAS installations, and always know definitively if the DAS is functioning correctly in the RF spectrum (and not just whether the hub/head-end is).

Implement real-time RF assurance models by monitoring users’ actual on-the-ground experience of your network, and know whether your SLAs are being met at any point in time.
