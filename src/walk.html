RANlytics Cerno Indoor DAS (Walk) Test Solution

Better RF data, collected and made available faster, combined with more powerful reporting and analysis tools – at lower cost

The Problems

Walk tests of buildings, and of sites such as sports stadiums, public spaces, recreational facilities and outdoor malls has always been time-consuming and costly; and the tests necessary to prepare for, and confirm the successful installation of a DAS often delay the project.

Legacy equipment is expensive and prone to faults, and equipment complexity means that tests have to be performed by experienced engineers. Typically, 20% or more of walk tests performed today require a second site visit to collect data that wasn’t initially collected because of equipment issues.

Post-processing of log files, and the subsequent manual and time-intensive collation of data and preparation of reports then consumes even more time and cost; whilst the burden on your skilled RF engineers to do this necessary but repetitive work both increases your costs and lowers team morale.

The RANlytics Solution

Cerno from RANlytics has been designed and built from the ground up to address each and every one of these issues and constraints.

Using our custom-built and highly reliable Cerno RF testers we can walk test far more quickly and more frequently. Because Cerno is so scalable, we can test all operator/technology/band combinations in a single pass. And because Cerno is so robust and reliable, follow-up site visits to collect missing data are eliminated.

We immediately upload log files to our cloud for instantaneous post-processing, and fully-automated generation of management-quality reports in MS Word, complete with plots and tabulated results.

Our customers can immediately see and analyse the data through our web-based Cerno Workbench – within minutes of the walk test being completed.

And because all data from all tests are always available, time-based change analysis is quick and easy – for the first time.
