RANlytics Cirrus and Cirrus-LT DAS Antenna Monitors

Monitor passive and active DAS antennas – individually, continuously and in real-time

The Problems

Verifying that every antenna, coupler and cable in a DAS installation has been installed and configured correctly is difficult, time-consuming & expensive; and ensuring that all elements continue to operate to spec once installed has been impossible.

Installers frequently have to revisit sites to rectify installation or equipment faults – incurring additional costs that compromise the financial viability of projects. Installation project managers rely exclusively on installers’ reports to know which elements have been installed successfully – reports that can’t be independently verified.

Then, once commissioned, there is no means of assuring that DAS installations are operating correctly; and, when an issue occurs, operators or building owners can’t identify which antenna or coupler has failed without an expensive site visit.

The RANlytics Solution

RANlytics has introduced the Cirrus and Cirrus-LT (for Long Term) antenna monitors to augment our drive test, walk test and static RF monitoring solutions with the industry’s first and only low-cost DAS antenna monitor.

A Cirrus monitor is placed in the ceiling space adjacent to each antenna. Installation does not require specialised or technical skills, nor even an electrician, and as Cirrus is battery-powered and fully autonomous, no cabling is necessary either.

A Cirrus base station injects a signal into the DAS and each Cirrus monitor periodically ‘wakes’ to check the strength of the signal from the antenna it is monitoring, so that over time, a path loss baseline is built and anomalies & failures – including in couplers & cables – can be identified and reported.

During deployment, the project manager can continuously monitor the progress of the installation, and can immediately identify a faulty or incorrectly-installed component – before the installers leave the site.

Then, once commissioned, Cirrus provides an ongoing service assurance capability, enabling you to proactively identify, and rapidly rectify, faults.
